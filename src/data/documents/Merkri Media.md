# Merkri Media

## What It Is
A brand building agency.

Never call it:
- Digital marketing agency
- Social media agency
- Marketing agency
- Content agency

Always: brand building agency.

## The Core Idea
Most B2B businesses are great at what they do
but invisible to the people who need them.
They're the best-kept secret in their industry.

Merkri Media fixes that. We help B2B founders
become the default name in their category —
the brand people think of first, not just
another option in the market.

The positioning concept: becoming the
"Fevicol of your industry" — when someone
thinks of your product category, they think
of you. That's the goal.
